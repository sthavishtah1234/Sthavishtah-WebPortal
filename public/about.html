About Us

Sthavishtah Yoga and Wellness was founded with a simple mission: to help people achieve physical and mental well-being through the ancient practice of yoga and modern wellness techniques.

Our name "Sthavishtah" is derived from Sanskrit, representing stability and strength - the core principles that guide our approach to wellness.

Our Philosophy

We believe that wellness is a holistic journey that encompasses the mind, body, and spirit. Our approach integrates traditional yoga practices with contemporary wellness methods to create a comprehensive path to health.

At Sthavishtah, we understand that each person's wellness journey is unique. That's why we offer flexible batches, personalized guidance, and a supportive community to help you achieve your goals.

Our Instructors

Our team consists of certified yoga instructors with years of experience in various yoga styles. They are passionate about sharing their knowledge and helping you transform your life through the power of yoga and mindfulness.

Each instructor brings their unique expertise and teaching style, ensuring that you receive well-rounded guidance on your wellness journey.

Join Our Community

Becoming a part of Sthavishtah means joining a supportive community of like-minded individuals who are committed to their wellness journey. We foster an environment of encouragement, learning, and growth.
